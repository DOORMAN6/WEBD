PARKS

The City of Duncan takes great pride in its parks. Green spaces promote healthy living and a sense of place and community as people walk, play, and
attend events.

Parks and green spaces reduce pollution, and provide a
venue for adults and youth to stay active. Exposing young people
to the joys of outdoor activities in open spaces such as parks helps mould future generations into individuals who are concerned about the environment, and therefore are committed
to revitalizing and enhancing the urban park system.

Green Spaces

There are grassy, treed green spaces throughout Centennial Heights, on Pine Avenue, and at Station Street Common, which is the perfect place to sit and have a break, while surrounded by a mural on one side and totem poles on the other.
